JeevanSetu – 24×7 Rural Healthcare Platform

JeevanSetu is an AI-powered telemedicine platform designed to provide accessible, affordable, and real-time healthcare services to rural and remote areas. It connects patients with doctors instantly and offers emergency support when it matters the most.

---

Features

- AI Symptom Checker for smart health analysis
- 24×7 Doctor Availability
- Emergency Support System (Ambulance and Alerts)
- Multilingual Support (Hindi and regional languages)
- Digital Health Profile (BP, weight, medical history, etc.)
- Optimized for low internet connectivity

---

How It Works

1. Create your health profile
2. Enter your symptoms (text or voice)
3. AI analyzes your condition
4. Connect to an available doctor
5. Use emergency support if required

---

Tech Stack

- Frontend: React (Lovable AI)
- Backend: Node.js with Express
- Database: Firebase Firestore

To bridge the healthcare gap in rural India by combining AI technology, real doctors, and emergency services into one accessible platform.
